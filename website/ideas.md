## Idea 1: "Deep Space Terminal" — Brutalist Cosmic

**Design Movement:** Brutalist Web + Cosmic Horror
**Core Principles:**
- Raw terminal aesthetics collide with deep space imagery
- Monospace fonts everywhere, like staring into a void
- Intentional visual noise — scanlines, static, glitch effects
- No pretense of polish; the distro is a WIP and the site shows it

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#050510) background with electric cyan (#00FFCC) and warning amber (#FFB800) accents. The colors of a terminal at 3am.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric, left-heavy. Content bleeds to edges. No padding pretense. Sections separated by raw `<hr>` styled as terminal dividers.

**Signature Elements:**
- Animated terminal cursor blinking throughout
- Glitch text effect on the title
- Scanline overlay on hero section

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states feel like selecting text in a terminal. Clicks feel like keystrokes.

**Animation:** Glitch flickers on headings, typewriter effect for taglines, subtle CRT scanline animation.

**Typography System:** JetBrains Mono for everything. Size hierarchy through weight and color, not font family changes.

## Idea 2: "Moonlit Nebula" — Dark Cosmic Elegance

**Design Movement:** Dark Luxury + Space Opera
**Core Principles:**
- Deep space as a canvas — stars, nebulae, cosmic gradients
- Elegant but haunted; beautiful but slightly broken
- Glassmorphism cards floating in the void
- Subtle animations that feel like drifting through space

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy-black (#04050F) base, violet-indigo gradient accents (#7C3AED → #3B82F6), soft moonlight white (#E8EEFF) text. The palette of a moonlit night.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with star field, then floating glassmorphism cards on dark sections. Vertical rhythm with generous spacing.

**Signature Elements:**
- Animated star field in hero (CSS/canvas)
- Glassmorphism feature cards with subtle border glow
- Moon phase emoji used as section dividers 🌑🌒🌓🌔🌕

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states glow softly like stars. Smooth parallax on scroll.

**Animation:** Twinkling stars, floating card entrance animations, smooth section transitions.

**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (headings) + JetBrains Mono (code/terminal elements). Bold display sizes for impact.

## Idea 3: "Ash's Panic Room" — Chaotic Developer Diary

**Design Movement:** Zine Culture + Developer Blog + Cosmic Chaos
**Core Principles:**
- Honest about the chaos — the site IS the lore
- Handwritten-feel elements mixed with terminal output
- The bait counter (98+) is a featured stat
- S3RL energy: loud, fast, unhinged but lovable

**Color Philosophy:** Dark charcoal (#111118) with hot pink (#FF2D78) and electric blue (#00B4FF) accents. The colors of a rave in space.

**Layout Paradigm:** Newspaper-style chaotic grid. Some sections are rotated slightly. Sticky notes aesthetic for feature cards.

**Signature Elements:**
- Live "Licznik przynęt" (bait counter) prominently displayed
- "NoVa V3 is dead. RIP." tombstone section
- Scrolling marquee of chaos quotes from the dev logs

**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything is slightly too fast. Hover effects are aggressive. The site feels like it's barely holding together — like the distro.

**Animation:** Fast, snappy transitions. Marquee text. Shake animation on the download button (it's okay, it's real this time).

**Typography System:** Bebas Neue (display) + Space Mono (body). Loud and unapologetic.
